Secondary Research

Secondary research involves analyzing existing data, studies, and reports to gather insights. By utilizing credible sources such as academic papers, industry reports, government publications, and online databases, secondary research provides a cost-effective and efficient way to understand trends, benchmarks, and market dynamics without conducting original research It’s an essential step in gaining a foundational understanding of your topic and supporting data-driven decisions.
